Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us.

How far in advance should I order my invitations?

We recommend booking your spot 6-8 months before your wedding date. Custom design and specialty printing (like letterpress or foil) typically take 6-10 weeks from the start of the design process to delivery.

Do you offer custom venue illustrations?

Yes! Custom watercolor or line-art illustrations of your venue are one of our specialties. They can be added to details cards, envelope liners, or even the main invitation itself.

Can I order a sample before committing?

Absolutely. We offer curated sample packs that showcase our paper weights, printing methods (digital, letterpress, foil), and various embellishments. You can purchase a sample pack directly from our shop.

Do you handle addressing and mailing?

Yes, we offer both digital recipient addressing and handwritten calligraphy options. We can also provide white-glove assembly and mailing services so you don't have to lift a finger.
