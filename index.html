Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about making and ordering your invitations.

Can I order a custom sample before buying the full set?

Yes! Once you finish designing your invitation online, you can choose to order a single personalized sample for $5. This allows you to see the paper quality and color accuracy before placing your large order.

Can I download the file to print locally?

Absolutely. If you prefer to print them yourself or use a local print shop, you can purchase the high-resolution digital download (PDF, JPG, or PNG with bleed marks) for a flat fee.

How does free guest addressing work?

It's simple! You'll be prompted to download our CSV spreadsheet template. Add your guest names and addresses, upload the file back into our editor, and choose a font. We will print the addresses directly on your envelopes for free.

Can I upload my own artwork or photos?

Yes, our editor supports image uploads. You can easily add engagement photos to your save the dates, or upload custom monograms and venue illustrations to any of our templates.
