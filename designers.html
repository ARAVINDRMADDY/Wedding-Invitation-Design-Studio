The Faces Behind The Platform

Meet Our Creators

Discover the talented independent artists, typography masters, and digital asset creators whose designs power our easy-to-use invitation maker.

"Software shouldn't mean sacrificing fine art."

Platform Visionary

Sarah Jenkins

Head of Digital Curation

Sarah built our marketplace with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between bespoke wedding art and intuitive DIY software. As a classically trained editorial designer, she reviews every template submitted to our platform.

She ensures that every drag-and-drop layout maintains perfect structural hierarchy, and that every digital font renders beautifully whether it's exported as a web evite or printed on luxury cotton paper.

When she isn't onboarding new independent artists to our marketplace, you can find Sarah hosting webinars on DIY wedding branding and typography.

The Asset Builders

Digital Artists & Illustrators

Meet the top contributors who hand-paint and digitize the massive library of transparent PNGs, floral borders, and vector icons available in our maker tool.

Isabella Fontaine

Digital Floral Illustrator

Isabella paints loose, romantic florals which she then scans at ultra-high resolutions. Her asset packs allow brides to drag-and-drop realistic watercolor bouquets directly onto their digital canvas.

David Lin

Vector & Layout Artist

David builds the scalable vector graphics (SVGs) used for our architectural frames, borders, and modern geometric layouts. His assets stay perfectly crisp no matter how much you resize them in the editor.

The Typography Engine

Font & Type Masters

A great invitation relies on incredible fonts. Our platform features exclusive web-safe fonts and dynamic text tools built by leading type designers, ensuring your digital text mimics real calligraphy.

Arthur Pendelton

Master Typographer

Arthur digitizes historic calligraphy scripts and converts them into smart OpenType web fonts with automatic ligatures and swashes that respond as you type in our editor.

Clara Hughes

Dynamic Monogram Architect

Clara engineered our proprietary monogram generator tool, allowing users to type two initials and instantly see dozens of interlocking, customizable crest designs.

The Software Experience

Engineering & Support Leads

Meet the technical team that ensures your digital designs export flawlessly and your physical print orders arrive looking exactly like your screen.

Amara Singh

Director of UX/UI

Amara leads the design of the studio maker interface. She focuses on making the drag-and-drop tools intuitive, ensuring even the least tech-savvy users can create magic.

James Worthington

Print Calibration Expert

James manages the bridge between our software and our print partners. He calibrates our PDF export engine so RGB screen colors translate perfectly to CMYK print inks.

Claire Dubois

Customer Success Lead

Got stuck on a layout? Claire's team is available via live chat to help you fix margins, align text, or advise on the best cardstock for your specific template choice.
