Wedding stationery etiquette can feel like a maze. We've simplified the most common questions so you can mail with confidence.

1

When to Mail Everything

Save the Dates: 6 to 8 months before the wedding (8 to 12 months for destination weddings).
Invitations: 6 to 8 weeks before the wedding (10 to 12 weeks for).

Addressing Inner Envelopes

The outer envelope is formal (Mr. and Mrs. John Smith), while the inner envelope is more intimate and specifies exactly who is invited (Uncle John and Aunt Mary, or Mr. Smith and Guest).

Read the addressing guide →
3

Registry Information

Traditional etiquette dictates that registry information should never be printed directly on the main invitation. Instead, include your wedding website URL on a small details card.

Handling the Plus
