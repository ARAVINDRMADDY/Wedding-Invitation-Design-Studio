About Design Studio

Discover the passion, people, and purpose behind our bespoke design studio. We believe that beautiful stationery sets the tone for your unforgettable day.

Our Guiding Light

The Philosophy of Print

We believe that holding a beautifully crafted piece of paper is a sensory experience. Our approach marries old-world printing techniques with modern, romantic design sensibilities.

Intentional Design

Every curve of calligraphy and choice of whitespace is made with deep intention. We don't just fill space; we curate it to let your story breathe.

Tactile Luxury

From thick, pillowy cotton cardstock to the subtle raised texture of thermography, we design for the hands just as much as for the eyes.

Collaborative Care

You aren't just a client; you're a co-creator. We guide you through etiquette, wording, and assembly to make the process completely stress-free.

The Unboxing

We consider the journey of the envelope. The way the flap opens, the reveal of the liner, and the first glimpse of the suite are all orchestrated moments.

The Ingredients of
Fine Stationery

An invitation is only as beautiful as the materials it's made from. We source only the finest heirloom-quality supplies globally.

Foundation

Handmade Paper

Deckle-edged, 100% cotton rag paper crafted in traditional mills. Every sheet is unique and soft to the touch.

Embellishment

Artisan Wax Seals

Custom brass stamps pressed into flexible, mailable sealing wax. Available in over 40 distinct, rich colorways.

Finishing Touch

Hand-Dyed Silk

Plant-dyed habotai and chiffon silk ribbons with frayed edges, delicately tied to hold your invitation suite together.

Behind The Scenes

Inside Our Creative Haven

Located in the heart of the historic arts district, our studio is a sun-drenched space filled with vintage printing presses, endless spools of ribbon, and walls covered in sketches and pantone swatches.

We invite local couples to visit our showroom to touch the papers and see the foil shining in person. For our international clients, we replicate this intimate experience through detailed digital consultations and express-shipped sample curations.
