Our Story

Built for Brides, Powered by Tech

Design Studio was born out of frustration. Our founders realized that couples were either forced to settle for generic, mass-produced invites or pay exorbitant fees for custom design work. There was no middle ground.

We brought together top-tier software engineers and award-winning graphic designers to build a platform that bridges that gap. Today, our SaaS platform handles millions of designs, making wedding planning just a little bit easier and much more beautiful.

A Digital-First Approach

By empowering couples to choose digital delivery (E-vites) alongside traditional print, our platform has helped save thousands of trees. When you do choose to print with us, we ensure it's done responsibly.

  • Seamless E-vite delivery via Email & SMS
  • Sustainable, FSC-certified printing partners
  • Consolidated shipping to reduce carbon footprint
